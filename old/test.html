Welcome to myMessage.io!

Ever wonder about how to store your message, information, file permanently? Something that is very important to you, some precious memory, picture, sound, video, even just plain message. You want to store it forever, even after you died, to pass it down to your children and grand children. How can you store something permanently? Pendrive? SSD Harddisk? Cloud storage? Multiple PC? On paper? On DVD? Yes, storing the information on above can last very long. But the problem is what if you lost it? A pendrive, SSD harddisk, physical storage, dvd can be lost, misplaced, stolen, after long period of time. It can lost during transit, damaged, stolen, etc. Pendrive can be faulty after sometime. Exposure to strong magnetic field also can damage it. What about cloud storage? Drop box, Google cloud, Microsoft Cloud, they are very reliable right? Yes, but hack always happen, it happened in the past, and it will happen in the future again. Your information stored in the cloud storage can be lost, modified, hacked, or deleted due to inactive for many years. So it is not permanent storage. Thus this is what this project is trying to solve. A very easy method to store your message/file/information permanently and very low cost. Just store and forget. Come back 1 year, 5 years,1 decade, 10 decades later to retrive your information. We store your messages on the blockchain. No one can delete, modifiy, and steal your information. If you don't want your information to be known public, you can encrypt it. No one can see your information without knowing the secret key. Cost, how much? It is very cheap, at $0.1 per message! Just pay once only! No need to pay yearly like cloud storage. And you can view or retrive your information anytime. Once your message is stored in the blockchain, it will be remained in the blockchain forever. Blockchain is immutable, undeletable. The message once stored it will remain there forever, no one can delete it, modify it.

What information you can store?

ASCII Text Art Encrypted message for sensitive information Pass messages between parties Lover vouch/declaration - like a lover lock Valentine message board A verified message from a verified source. A will message (non legal). A will inheritance of crypto assets. Patents? Music rhythm? Songs? Idea? Shoutout to commend employee/staffs/co-workers Appreciate thanks note, etc

How does it works?

A smart contract is deployed in Zilliqa network, a front end dapp is used to interact with the smart contract. You then type the message or attach a file, encrypt it if necessary and then send to the network, pay some fee. That's all.

How do I retrieve my message? Is it secured?

You can retrieve the message by txid. Or you can search using keywords. The encryption is very secured, using AES encryption algorithm.
